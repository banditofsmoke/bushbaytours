Welcome to Bush & Bay Tours

We are a passionate team of Tourism, Hospitality, and Conservation professionals dedicated to showcasing the breathtaking beauty of Nelson Mandela Bay and the surrounding Eastern Cape bushveld areas. As South Africa's Adventure Province, we offer unforgettable experiences that will leave you in awe of nature's wonders.

Our commitment is to provide you with seamless, comfortable, and informative tours. We'll collect you from your accommodation in the Port Elizabeth/Gqeberha area in our modern, air-conditioned vehicles. Sit back and relax as our qualified guides and rangers take you on scenic journeys filled with fascinating insights into our region's rich biodiversity and cultural heritage.

At Bush & Bay Tours, we understand that every traveler is unique. That's why we offer customizable itineraries to match your interests and schedule. Whether you're seeking thrilling wildlife encounters, serene coastal views, or immersive cultural experiences, we have the perfect adventure waiting for you. Join us and discover the magic of the Eastern Cape!

Our Exciting Tours

Addo National Park Full Day Tour

Embark on a full-day adventure in South Africa's third-largest national park. Witness the majesty of the Big 5 and countless other species in their natural habitat.

Highlights:

  • 8 AM pickup from your accommodation
  • Air-conditioned vehicle transport
  • Expert guide throughout the tour
  • Refreshment stops at scenic vantage points
  • Delicious lunch in the heart of the park

Cost per person: R2550

Addo National Park Half Day Tour

Perfect for those short on time but big on adventure. Experience the wonders of Addo in a compact 4-hour tour.

Highlights:

  • Flexible morning or afternoon departures
  • Chance to spot the Big 5
  • Knowledgeable guide to enhance your experience
  • Refreshment stop included

Cost per person: R1750

Addo Nat Park & Private Game Reserve Full Day and Evening Tour

The ultimate safari experience combining Addo National Park and a private game reserve, including a night drive.

Highlights:

  • Full day in Addo National Elephant Park
  • Lunch at an iconic spot
  • Afternoon and sunset game drive in a private reserve
  • Dinner under the stars in a thatched boma
  • Thrilling night game drive

Cost per person: R3750

Marine Safari

Discover the wonders of Nelson Mandela Bay, a paradise for marine life enthusiasts.

Highlights:

  • Bottlenose Dolphin spotting
  • Visit to bay islands (viewed from the boat)
  • Chance to see whales (seasonal)
  • Visit to Cape Recife Coastal Conservancy
  • Tour of penguin rehabilitation center

Cost per person: R2950

City Tour

Explore the rich history and culture of Port Elizabeth/Gqeberha on this enlightening city tour.

Highlights:

  • Visit to Fort Frederick
  • Exploration of the Donkin Reserve
  • Tour of the South End Museum
  • Visit to Cape Recife Lighthouse
  • Tour of penguin rehabilitation center

Cost per person: R950
